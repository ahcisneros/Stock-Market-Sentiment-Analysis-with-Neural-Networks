A **Sentiment analysis** refers to the use of natural language processing, text analysis, computational linguistics, and biometrics to systematically identify, extract, quantify, and study affective states and subjective information. `Twitter-LSTM-Sentiment-Anaysis` is a LSTM (Long Short Term) artificial recurrent neural network architecture used in the field of deep learning. 

<h1 align="center"><img src="Images/LSTM.png" width="800" align="center"></h1>

Unlike standard feedforward neural networks, LSTM has feedback connections. It can not only process single data points, but also entire sequences of data. We are attempting to derive emotions from tweets regarding assets in our portfolio. Find out more about our fund, <a href="https://www.plutus21.com/">Plutus21 Capital</a>.
